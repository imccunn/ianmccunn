Right-Hand Picking Pattern Generator

for guitar right-hand warmup, exercise and creativity.

Directions: Select the type of picking (fingerstyle or hybrid picking). Enter the number of attacks per pattern and number of patterns, then click 'Generate'.

Use Cases and Possible Practice Exercises
  1. Practice single patterns by assigning fingers to strings and alternating fingers based on the given pattern.
  2. Apply accents and different attack types to specific fingers/notes at vaious divisions and sub-divisions.
  3. Improvise the position of chord changes based on a right-hand attack.
Note: By selecting hybrid picking, 'i' will be excluded. In this case, 'p' can be considered to be your pick.
